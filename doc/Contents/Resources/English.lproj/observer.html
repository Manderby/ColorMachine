Observer

The norm-valence spectra are chosen according to the selection of the observer.

Most color computations are performed with the 2 Degree observer from 1931. Following are the choices built-in to Color Machine:

  • CIE 1931 2 deg: The spectral data provided by www.cie.co.at for the 1931 2 degree observer.
  • CIE 1964 10 deg: The spectral data provided by www.cie.co.at for the 1964 10 degree observer.
  • Judd 1951 2 deg: The data provided by www.cvrl.org for the correction made by Judd of the 1931 2 degree observer.
  • Judd Voss 1978 2 deg: The data provided by www.cvrl.org for the correction made by Judd and Voss of the 1931 2 degree observer.
