About

Tobias Stamm, the author of Color Machine, has been working in color research and in the printing industry for many years. During his work as a software-engineer, a growing collection of highly verified color transformations has been implemented which finally resulted in a color-library named Color Machine Library (CML). The Application Color Machine is an application showcasing the potential of the library with a colorful GUI.

Tobias Stamm has founded its own software-company Manderim GmbH in September 2011 and started publishing the application under the company name.
